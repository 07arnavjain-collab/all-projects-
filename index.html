01 / About

Who Am I?

I am Arnav Jain, a second-year B.Tech student in Mathematics & Computing at JIIT Noida, deeply passionate about building technology that matters. At my core, I love solving complex problems and believe that the best software doesn't just function — it empowers people.

My journey so far has involved building impactful platforms like Nagrik Mesh, a real-time civic engagement tool, and serving as the Head of the Election Commission for 2,000+ students at DPS Indirapuram.

Looking ahead, my absolute focus is on becoming a versatile AI Generalist. I am actively deep-diving into the intricate layers of Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, and Generative/Agentic AI. My goal is to orchestrate these cutting-edge technologies to architect intelligent systems that solve profound, real-world challenges at scale.

Beyond technology, I am fascinated by Geopolitics & International Affairs, and you will often find me competing on a badminton court.
